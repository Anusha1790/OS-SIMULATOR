Disk Scheduling



Comparing different algorithms
The different algorithms are:
  1. FCFS - First Come First Serve
  2. SSTF - Shortest Seek Time First
  3. SCAN - also referred as the Elevator algorithm
  4. CSCAN - Circular SCAN
  5. LOOK - modified version of SCAN
  6. CLOOK - modified version of CSCAN
