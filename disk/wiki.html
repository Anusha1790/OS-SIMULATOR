Disk Scheduling


Disk scheduling is is done by operating systems to schedule I/O requests arriving for disk. Disk scheduling is also known as I/O scheduling.
There are mainly 6 types of Disc scheduling examined here.They are
1. FCFS(First Come First Serve)
2. SSTF(shortest seek time first)
3. SCAN
4. C-SCAN
5. LOOK
6. C-LOOK
All these schedulings are examined and Total Head Movements are Compared.
